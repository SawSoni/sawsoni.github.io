Singapore & Cruise

(7Nights / 8Days)

Day 1: Singapore

Day 1: Singapore Arrive at Singapore, clear immigration formalities and meet our representative at arrival lounge. Freshen-up at Airport & further transfer to hotel for Check-In. Evening proceed to Night Safari. The Night Safari is the world's first nocturnal zoo and currently houses over 2,500 animals representing over 130 species, of which 38% are threatened species. Return back at Night. Overnight in Singapore. (Meals: Lunch & Dinner at Indian Restaurant

Day 2: Singapore

Day 2: Singapore After breakfast proceed for a Panoramic TOUR OF SINGAPORE (Drive pass the Suntech city, Parliament house, supreme court, city hall, china town, little India, Merlion Park) & Singapore Flyer. Later in the evening visit GARDEN BY THE BAY, the Flower Dome replicates the cool-dry climate of Mediterranean regions and houses a collection of plants from deserts all over the world. Move further to Marina Bay Sands Observation Deck. Return back late evening. Overnight at Singapore. (Meals: Breakfast at Hotel, Lunch & Dinner at Indian Restaurant)

Day 3: Singapore

Day 3: Singapore After breakfast we proceed towards Sentosa Island for full day fun. Enjoy 20 Attractions Extreme Pass which includes one way by Cable Car, Madame Tussauds wax museum, SEA Aquarium, Wings of Time Show, 4D Adventure Land, Butterfly & Insect Kingdom, Luge Segway Bicycle Rides Etc. Return back late evening. Overnight at Singapore. (Meals: Breakfast at Hotel & Dinner at Indian Restaurant)

Day 4: Singapore

Day 4: Singapore After breakfast proceed to proceed to UNIVERSAL STUDIOS. Enter the thrilling world of movie magic as you “ride the movies®” on roller coasters and other movie-themed attractions. Be dazzled and delighted by an immersive entertainment experience that brings the silver screen to life. Return back in the evening. Overnight in Singapore. (Meals: Breakfast at Hotel & Dinner at Indian Restaurant)

Day 5: Singapore

Day 5: Singapore After breakfast check-out from Hotel & proceed towards Jurong Bird Park, a world-famous bird zoo where there are specimens of magnificent bird life from around the world, including a large flock of flamingos. It is currently the world's largest bird park in terms of the number of birds, and second largest both in the number of bird species and land area (after Germany's Weltvogelpark Walsrode). There are 5,000 birds of 400 species in Jurong Bird Park, of which 29 are of threatened species. Later transfer to cruise terminal to board your cruise. (Meals: Breakfast at Hotel & Lunch at Indian Restaurant)
